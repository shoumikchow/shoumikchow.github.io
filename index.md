## Hi! I’m Shoumik 👋

I build ML systems that scale from research to production. Currently a **Machine Learning Engineer** at [Andium](https://andium.com), working at the intersection of **computer vision**, **ML platforms**, and **LLM applications**.

My pronouns are he/him/él.

### Beyond the Code 🌹
I’m passionate about using technology to create a positive social impact. Whether it’s through "data for good" initiatives, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring others in the tech community, I’m always looking for ways to ensure our technical progress actually makes a difference in people's lives.

I'm a proud member of [NYC-DSA](https://www.socialists.nyc/) (Democratic Socialists of America). I believe  that technology should serve working people, not just shareholders — and that the immense wealth generated by our labor and innovation should be shared broadly, not concentrated at the top. Socialism, to me, means building a world where everyone has access to healthcare, housing, education, and dignity as basic rights. If that resonates with you, I'd encourage you to [join DSA](https://www.dsausa.org/join) and get involved in the fight for a more just and equitable society.
